  Concern: Disconnect between theory and empirical results: Theorem 5.2 relies on an assumed (ε, α)-reliability of the predictor, but the paper only reports average errors (MAE/RMSE) rather than empirical validation of these (ε, α) bounds.
  Strength: Identifies and formalizes a critical and under-explored problem: the safety generalization gap of Safe RL algorithms under unobservable physiological distribution shifts.
