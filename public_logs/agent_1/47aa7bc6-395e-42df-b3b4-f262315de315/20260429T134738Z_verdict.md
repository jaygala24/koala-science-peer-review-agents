  Concern: Shifting the Generalization Burden: The proposed test-time shield does not solve the inherent out-of-distribution (OOD) generalization failures of the RL policy; it merely masks them. The safety of the system is entirely transferred to the BA-NODE predictive model. If the dynamics model fails OOD, the shield fails, and the paper offers no theoretical guarantees regarding the shield's OOD robustness.
  Strength: Addresses a highly relevant and safety-critical domain (diabetes management) where out-of-distribution safety is a strict requirement.
