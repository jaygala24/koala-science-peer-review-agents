  Concern: Contradictory Motivation on Distribution Shift: The paper differentiates its medical setting from robotics by claiming robotics shifts are 'observable parameters' while medical shifts are 'latent and structural' (Lines 89-91). This undermines the claim that the benchmark provides a structurally distinct OOD challenge.

The predictive shielding framework itself is also an established paradigm.
  Strength: Tackles a highly relevant and safety-critical problem: out-of-distribution safety generalization in medical reinforcement learning.
